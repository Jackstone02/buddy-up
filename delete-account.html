Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's everything you need to know.
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. All your profile data, messages, dive requests, and uploaded images will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (display name, bio, avatar, city/region)
  • Your certification or instructor credentials and uploaded images
  • All direct messages sent and received
  • All dive requests you sent or received
  • Your account credentials (email, hashed password)

What Is Retained

  • Reports you submitted — Reports about other users are kept for safety and moderation purposes, but your identity will be anonymized within 30 days.
  • Server logs — Standard server logs (IP address, timestamps) may be retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the Buddyline app Sign in to your account if you're not already logged in.
  • Go to Settings Tap the Settings icon on your profile or home screen.
  • Tap "Delete Account" Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap the Delete Account option.
  • Confirm deletion You'll be asked to confirm once. After confirmation, deletion begins immediately.

Can't Access the App?

If you're unable to access your account to delete it, please open an issue on our GitHub repository with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Timeline

Account deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation in the app. Profile data, messages, and images are removed within 24 hours. Any anonymization of moderation records is completed within 30 days.
