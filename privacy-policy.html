Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026
Buddyline is a safety-focused freediving community app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Who We Are

Buddyline ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application designed to connect certified freedivers with dive buddies and freediving instructors. We are committed to responsible data practices and comply with applicable privacy laws.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data: Email address, display name, and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth).
  • Profile data: Role (beginner / certified / instructor), city/region, bio, avatar image, certification level, agency, years of experience, and disciplines.
  • Verification data: Certification card images and instructor credential images (stored in Supabase Storage).
  • Messages: Content of direct messages sent between users within the app.
  • Dive requests: Requested date, location name, disciplines, and any notes attached to a dive request.
  • Reports & blocks: Reason and details of user reports; block relationships between users.
  • Usage data: Standard server logs (IP address, timestamp, device type) collected automatically by Supabase and Expo.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and authenticate your account.
  • To display your profile to other verified users in search results.
  • To facilitate messaging and dive request coordination between users.
  • To verify instructor and certified diver credentials before granting full access.
  • To enforce safety and community standards (reports, blocks, age verification).
  • To send transactional emails (email confirmation, password reset) via Supabase.
  • To improve the app and diagnose technical issues.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Other users: Your public profile (display name, city/region, bio, avatar, cert level, disciplines) is visible to authenticated app users based on your role permissions.
  • Supabase: Our backend provider. Data is stored in Supabase's hosted PostgreSQL and Storage services. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect user safety.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Deleted accounts are removed from the app within 30 days. Certification and credential images are deleted when your account is deleted or when you upload a replacement.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data via the Profile Edit screen in the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data — see our Account Deletion page.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

7. Age Requirement

Buddyline is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We require users to confirm their age at signup. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately deleted.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth and never stored in plaintext. Certification images are stored in private Supabase Storage buckets accessible only to the authenticated user and admins.

9. Cookies & Tracking

The Buddyline mobile app does not use cookies. The GitHub Pages website (this page) does not use tracking cookies or analytics.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated in-app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
